Build receipt and delivery note apps: one-click receipt creation from invoice with partial payment tracking, one-click delivery note creation from quotation/invoice, detail pages with editable fields and status actions, and PDF templates for both document types.

Output: Working /apps/receipt and /apps/delivery-note routes with one-click creation, detail pages, PDFs, and status workflows.

    - Update document with new paymentMethod, paymentDate, paidAmount
    - Recalculate if parent invoice should be "paid": sum all non-voided receipt amounts for source invoice, compare to invoice total
    - revalidatePath
